Who am I?

I'm born and raised in Orlando, Florida, and have lived here nearly all my life. I've lived in nearly every corner of East Orlando, with the theme parks right in my backyard. I find inspiration in new technologies, and how they shape the world around us.

What's my background?

I come from a backround of people: hospitality, customer service, and customer experience. After setting a goal to teach myself web development in 2017, I graduated from UCF's Full-Stack Web Development program in Spring 2018.

What do I want to do?

I work with a full MERN JavaScript stack, but open to learning new languages and frameworks. I prefer back-end development, but I also enjoy front-end work as well. You can see some of the work I've done below.
